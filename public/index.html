About
COVID-19 has challenged each and every one of us in ways we could not have imagined a month ago. We may be separated physically, but we are all in this together. Some days leave us frustrated as we struggle with adapting so much of our lives online. Other days leave us upset about the reality of what is happening. Still other days leave us proud of the brave first responders who make enormous sacrifices for us all. Each day is a roller coaster of emotions. Yet we are all on that roller coaster together. For those of us stuck at home, we are doing the right thing, yet it can feel helpless and lonely. Those people might be thinking, “someone, anyone, tell me… something…”

This webpage is designed to help us document all of these emotions. An attempt to seek comfort in the act of sharing this experience. Perhaps you feel a wave of sorrow, and hearing that others are sad as well might bring you solace. Or maybe you want to hear funny stories to put a smile on your face again. Or you want to share a story to document this unique time in our lives. I hope you enjoy reading and sharing all the funny, sad, and beautiful stories that will live here.
- Eva Philips, April, 4, 2020
